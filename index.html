About Me

I am a Maryland based Producer and DJ and have always been fueled by my passion for music. Through my devotion and love for the craft, my ability to create music and show it to the world contains no boundaries. With a Bachelors of Science Degree in Music Production from Full Sail University, I have worked on an array of music with an influence from Hip-Hop, Electronic, Jazz and more. Forever trying to reinvent sound, I am an artist truly dedicated to the heart of music.
